City Rules

  1. Maintain public cleanliness and dispose of waste properly.
  2. Do not litter on streets, parks, or public spaces.
  3. Obey all traffic signals and road signs.
  4. Park vehicles only in designated parking areas.
  5. Respect public property and avoid vandalism.
  6. Refrain from making loud noise after 10 PM.
  7. Pay municipal taxes and bills on time.
  8. Follow building and construction regulations.
  9. Use water and electricity responsibly.
  10. Report any emergencies to local authorities immediately.
