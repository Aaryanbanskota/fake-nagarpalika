Proudly serving the community

Birtamode Negarpalika (Municipality) is a vibrant and growing city located in the Jhapa District of Province No. 1, Nepal. It serves as a key commercial and cultural hub in the region. Our mission is to foster sustainable community development by providing excellent services, infrastructure, and opportunities for all citizens. We embrace modernity while preserving our rich cultural heritage.

Explore our website to learn more about how Birtamode Negarpalika works for you, find important updates, and connect with your local government.
